What is Norovirus?

Norovirus is a highly contagious virus that causes stomach and intestinal inflammation, leading to symptoms like nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, and stomach cramps. It spreads easily through contaminated food, water, surfaces, and person-to-person contact. Norovirus is the leading cause of gastroenteritis worldwide, affecting people of all ages.

Symptoms of Norovirus

  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Stomach cramps or pain
  • Mild fever, chills, headache, and muscle aches

How It Spreads

  • Contaminated food and water
  • Close contact with infected individuals
  • Touching contaminated surfaces and then touching the mouth

Prevention Tips

  • Wash hands thoroughly with soap and water
  • Clean and disinfect surfaces frequently
  • Avoid consuming food or water prepared by someone ill
